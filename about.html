About Me

I'm Drew Kanuha, a student graphic designer who loves building visual systems that feel intentional and welcoming. I focus on typography, layout, and brand identity that balances clarity with personality.

I'm currently studying design while exploring projects that blend print, digital, and motion. My education keeps me curious about how design can simplify complex ideas and create stronger connections between people and brands.

I believe great design is equal parts strategy and emotion. I bring a collaborative mindset to every project, aiming for work that is polished, thoughtful, and ready for the real world.
